## Why Dify Was Not Called

The runtime did not expose a daily-report specific workflow that could be safely
identified and executed from the approved AI-DEV-047 flow. The existing generic
AI-DEV draft workflow is not sufficient for the daily-report contract because it
may not preserve the AI-DEV-045 input shape, forecast horizons, channel split,
review-required flags, and report safety boundaries.

The safe outcome was to stop before a Dify call. This avoided accidental use of
an unrelated workflow, accidental credential use, or a malformed daily-report
prompt.
